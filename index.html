About Eat My Fry

The spirit of quick food traces back to the bustling souqs of Arabia, where vendors served warm, ready-to-eat meals to travelers and traders.

HALAL CERTIFIED - HALAL CERTIFIED -

Eat My Fry

A Saudi Touch in Every Bite.

Welcome to Eat My Fry, a proudly Saudi-based, 100% halal certified Quick Service Restaurant (QSR) dedicated to elevating the fried chicken experience across the Kingdom and beyond. Rooted in Saudi values of hospitality and community, we aim to bring bold flavor, consistent quality, and warm service to every neighborhood. By expanding into cities and growing towns alike, we’re making premium, freshly prepared meals accessible to families and communities throughout Saudi Arabia.



From crispy fried chicken and tender wings to burgers, wraps, and value-packed combo meals, our menu is crafted to satisfy every craving. We use responsibly sourced poultry, authentic spices inspired by regional preferences, and careful preparation that reflects our commitment to excellence. Whether you’re grabbing a quick snack, enjoying a family meal, or spending time with friends, Eat My Fry delivers trusted flavor with a truly Saudi touch.
